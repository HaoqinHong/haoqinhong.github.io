Welcome to my homepage! I am Providence / 洪少观:

I am a first-year Integrated M.Phil - Ph.D Candidate at the School of AI & DS, USTC, advised by Prof. Jingrun Chen, and I also collaborate with associate researcher Congcong Zhu. If everything goes well, I will transfer to the USTC - CityUHK Joint Ph.D Degree Program in Fall 2027, co-advised by Prof. Gerhard Petrus Hancke and Prof. Rynson W. H. Lau at CityUHK. I received my B.Eng degree in Computer Science and Technology from Yuan Longping Honors Program at SWU in 2025.

I did research internships at cutting-edge research groups such as UBISOFT La Forge and iFlyTek Spark-LLM. I am also active in AI and robotics competitions, currently holding the Kaggle Competitions Expert title, and previously served as the algorithm leader and project manager of the SWU RoboMaster Lab.

My research interests focus on Physical Constitutive Representation Learning (Physical Intelligence & Interactive World Model). I am seeking research collaborations, feel free to exchange ideas.
