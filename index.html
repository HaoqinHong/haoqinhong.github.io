Welcome to my homepage! 🥰

I am a first-year academic master student in Artificial Intelligence at the School of AI & DS, University of Science and Technology of China, supervised by NSFC Distinguished Young Scholars Prof. Jingrun Chen, and I will also collaborate with Associate Researcher Congcong Zhu. I earned a B.Eng in Computer Science (Top Talent Program) in 2025 at Southwest University, under the guidance of Prof. Xiaofang Hu.

I did research internships at companies or institutes such as UBISOFT La Forge and iFlyTek Spark-LLM Team. I am also active in AI & Robotics competitions, currently a Kaggle Expert, and previously served as the algorithm leader and project manager of the SWU RoboMaster Team. I have published 2 first-author papers at conferences or journals such as AAAI 2026 and IJCNN & WCCI 2024, and have also published 2 co-author papers, such as in Neurocomputing 2025. I am currently seeking for research collaborations, feel free to exchange our ideas!

    My research interests focus on:
  • Physically Interactive World Models
  • Feed-Forward & Generative 3D Vision
  • Multimodal Learning & Robotics Perception
